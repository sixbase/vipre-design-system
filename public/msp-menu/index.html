MSP Menu — framework-agnostic

Everything to the left is the menu: the exact vds-sidenav markup from the design system, styled by vipre.css, with behavior wired by msp-menu.js (no framework, no dependencies).

Try it: click product cards to open/close, click a leaf row to move the active state, and hit Collapse at the bottom — then hover the icons to see the collapsed-rail tooltips.

To reuse: copy the three files (index.html markup, vipre.css, msp-menu.js). See README.md for React / Vue / Angular / plain-HTML recipes and the full behavior contract.
